// Who I Am

Grounded insight.
Precise execution.

I'm a Central Arkansas-based digital strategist with a proven track record across marketing, social media, data analysis, and entrepreneurship. Through Metcalf.Solutions, I help businesses cut through noise with campaigns and analysis built on what actually works — not what looks good in a slide deck.

My approach is field-tested: decades of hands-on observation across Arkansas weather patterns, local markets, and consumer behavior have sharpened an instinct for signal versus noise. I bring that same analytical rigor to digital strategy — whether that's building a social audience, diagnosing a funnel, or synthesizing raw data into a decision framework.

Recent work spans insurance sector digital presence, a real-time geopolitical intelligence briefing platform, and a rigorous field editorial on Windows 11 backup solutions — each demonstrating a different dimension of strategic communication and information design.
